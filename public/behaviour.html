Behaviour

Does this mean manatees can get in trouble?

We are not talking about bad behaviour like at school. When we talk about behaviour with animals we're simply talking about what they do!

Manatee behaviour facts

  • Apart from mothers with their young, or males following a receptive female, manatees are generally solitary animals. This means they don't have many friends :-(
  • Manatees are capable of understanding discrimination tasks and show signs of complex associative learning!
  • Manatees emit a wide range of sounds used in communication, especially between cows (parents) and their calves (babies).
