Manatees in popular culture

What does this mean?

In this page you can read about where you may have seen manatees before! This might be in books and films, or even in the news or stories you have heard!

Manatee habitat facts

  • The manatee has been linked to folklore on mermaids.
  • The famous explorer and scientist Jacques Cousteau actually made a film about manatees, title ‘the forgotten mermaids’!
  • In the olden days a very famous book about whales called Moby Dick was written. Sadly manatees were not the star of the show, however the author did describe them!
  • In the movie 51st state Samuel L Jackson befriended a manatee called Chris.
  • Sadly no manatees appeared in the film 50 first dates, however there were vomiting walruses which can sometimes be mistaken for manatees.
