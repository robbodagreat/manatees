Habitat

What is a habitat?

A habitat is where an animal lives, and also describes the condition it lives in

Manatee habitat facts

  • Manatees inhabit shallow, marshy coastal areas and rivers
  • They can be found in the Caribbean Sea, the Gulf of Mexico, the Amazon basin, and West Africa.
  • The main cause of death for manatees is humans! Human activity, such as large boats passing through where manatees live, can cause great damage to their homes and sometimes the manatees themselves.
