What is anatomy?

This talks about the bodies of manatees!

Manatee anatomy facts

  • Although they live underwater, manatees don't swallow much water so their insides are dry!
  • Manatee teeth are unique in that they are replaced horizontally, as opposed to vertically as in most other mammals.
  • Manatees live in both fresh and salt water. Most animals can only live in one or the other!
