Mating

Are we talking about how to be mates with a manatee?

When we talk about mating in the animal kingdom, we are talking about making babies!

Manatee mating facts

  • A baby manatee is called a calf.
  • Manatees usually only have one baby at a time, like humans.
  • Manatees usually only breed once every two years.
