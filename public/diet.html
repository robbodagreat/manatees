Diet

Does this mean manatees are trying to lose weight?

No, this isn't like your mum and dad going to weight watchers! When we talk about diets with animals, we are simply discussing what they usually eat.

Manatee diet facts

  • Manatees are herbivores! Their diet consists of more than 60 different under water plants!
  • Some favourite plants of the manatee are: floating hyacinth, pickerel weed, alligator weed, water lettuce, hydrilla, water celery, musk grass, mangrove leaves.
